In a Nut shell

Early days

Born and brought up in Mumbai, Shravya wanted to have quite a happening career since childhod. She aspired to be a cardiac surgeon.But the love for technology over-powered her decision between Biology and Computer Science. Securing a 100% score she proved that she's the master of her genre. Long into her Engineering degree she developed a love for 'organizing events'. She pursued a degree in MBA from IIM-A. Bundled with all the skills of tech and management and her craze for yummy food, she set up her first restaurant 'Hunger's Haven' in 2015.

Michelin-starred restaurants

In 2016, Shravya set up a cafe 'Rivera'. Being an avid reader, she came up with the concept of a library-cum-cafe which led to her new venture 'The Reader's Nest'. She has been awarded the highest degree of honor for her restaurants, all her restaurants have been awarded three Michelin stars. As a business woman she aspires to reach new heights, while remaining true to her principles and a grounded person.
